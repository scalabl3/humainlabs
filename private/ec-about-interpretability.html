Interpretability: Do I Fit?

The Role

Responsibilities

  1. Partner with a research lead on direction, project planning and execution, hiring, and people development
  2. Set and maintain a high bar for execution speed and quality, including identifying improvements to processes that help the team operate effectively
  3. Coach and support team members to have more impact and develop in their careers
  4. Drive the team's recruiting efforts, including hiring planning, process improvements, and sourcing and closing
  5. Help identify and support opportunities for collaboration with other teams across Anthropic
  6. Communicate team updates and results to other teams and leadership

You may be a good fit if you:

  1. Are an experienced manager (minimum 3-5 years) with a track record of effectively leading highly technical research and/or engineering teams
  2. Actively enjoy people management
  3. Have managed technical teams through periods of high ambiguity and change
  4. Are a quick learner, capable of understanding and contributing to discussions on complex technical topics
  5. Have experience with our research or are motivated to learn more about it
  6. Believe that advanced AI systems could have a transformative effect on the world, and are passionate about helping make sure that transformation goes well

Strong candidates may also have:

  1. A background in machine learning, AI, or a related technical field
  2. Experience scaling engineering infrastructure
  3. Strong people management experience, including coaching, performance evaluation, mentorship, and career development
  4. Excellent project management skills, including prioritization and cross-functional coordination
  5. Experience recruiting talent for your team including predicting staffing needs, sourcing candidates, designing interview loops, evaluating and interviewing candidates, and closing offers
  6. Excellent written and spoken communication and interpersonal skills
  7. Experience working on open-ended, exploratory research agendas aimed at foundational insights
